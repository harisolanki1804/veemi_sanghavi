Welcome to my world of spiritual exploration and Vedic wisdom!

As a spiritual seeker, author, and entrepreneur, I have dedicated my life to studying and sharing the ancient wisdom of Indian scriptures. With a strong foundation in various spiritual schools in India (Bharat), I have developed a unique content base that aims to positively break the generational cycle and empower future generations.

As a pragmatic guide, I offer valuable insights and practical advice on incorporating Ayurveda and Naturopathy into daily life, particularly for mothers who want to feed their families the right food for optimal health and well-being. My notebook on this subject is a treasure trove of knowledge, distilled from extensive research and experience.

My journey into the world of Vedic scriptures began after a successful career in International Business, Management, Marketing, Internet Technology, and Financial Markets, where I earned multiple degrees and diplomas. However, my true passion lay in studying and disseminating Indian scriptures, leading me to create hinduscriptures.com. This website is a testament to my expertise in technology and management, combined with my love for Vedic knowledge. My mission is to share the knowledge of Vedic scriptures with the world, and I am deeply committed to this cause. My book series, "Hindu Culture and Lifestyle," available on Amazon and Flipkart, provides valuable insights into Hindu culture for a global audience.

​ My efforts have been recognized with numerous awards, including the prestigious Hind Rattan Award and Pravasi Bhartiya Award. Join me on this journey of spiritual exploration and discovery, and let my wisdom guide you on your own path to self-discovery and growth.
